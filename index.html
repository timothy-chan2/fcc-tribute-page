Chris Hadfield

Chris Hadfield is:
  • a retired Canadian astronaut
  • the first Canadian to walk in space
  • served as the commander of the ISS
  • walked in space to help install the Canadarm2

For more information about Chris Hadfield, please click the link to his Wikipedia page.
